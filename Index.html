About Me

Israel Olumuyiwa

I'm a direct-response copywriter and creative strategist based in Lagos, specializing in DTC ecommerce and health/wellness supplement brands.

Before I write a word, I ask what a salesperson would ask: who is this customer, what do they already believe, and what's stopping them from buying right now. That's the lens — market sophistication, awareness level, the actual mechanism behind the claim — not just "does this sound good."

I study how big brands actually sell, and I write from the same place: copy is a sales conversation, not a writing exercise.

I'm early in my client work, which means every project gets the attention a 7–8 figure account would get from someone still proving it.
